## Problem

## Solution
- ESG‑aligned QA dashboards
- Predictive cooling optimization
- Renewable energy integration
- Audit‑ready compliance reporting

## Impact
- Reduced carbon footprint
- Lower energy costs
- Sustainable resilience
- Transparent ESG compliance
