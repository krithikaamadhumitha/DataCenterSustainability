# Sustainability in Data Center

## Overview
Re‑imagined data center operations with ESG‑embedded QA dashboards, predictive cooling optimization, renewable energy integration, and compliance reporting.

## Problem
Data centers face high energy costs, cooling inefficiencies, and ESG compliance challenges.

## Solution
- ESG‑aligned QA dashboards
- Predictive cooling optimization
- Renewable energy integration
- Audit‑ready compliance reporting

## Impact
- Reduced carbon footprint
- Lower energy costs
- Sustainable resilience
- Transparent ESG compliance
